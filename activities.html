Zero-gravity Sports

At Cosmic Oasis, we have a wide range of unique sports found nowhere else. Challenge your instincts with Zero-Gravity Frisbee, lose yourself in a multi-level laser tag arena with no floors or ceilings, or step into Spaceball; our signature zero-gravity sport where the ball moves in any direction, goals exist at every angle, and the rules of gravity no longer apply.

Spacewalks

Cosmic Oasis maintains a state-of-the-art spacewalking program built around guest safety and unforgettable experience in equal measure. Our airlocks, suits, and trained guides are designed to make venturing outside the hull as seamless as it is extraordinary.

Stargazing

Cosmic Oasis features dedicated stargazing areas positioned and designed specifically to maximize unobstructed views of deep space. Our onboard astronomers are available to guide your experience; helping you find constellations, planets, and phenomena that most people never get the chance to witness firsthand.
