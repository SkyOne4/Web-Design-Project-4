Artificial Gravity

Our hotel ring continuously and gently rotates, this creates a very natural feeling, very similar to Earth’s gravity. You can walk, eat, and sleep normally with your feet firmly on the floor. The station is large and spins slowly, so most guests feel no dizziness or motion sickness at all. Guests who want to feel and experience zero-gravity will have the opportunity to do so in designated zero-gravity locations.

Safety

The U.S. Space Surveillance Network constantly tracks objects as small as 10 cm and works directly with us, keeping the hotel in a safe orbit. In the extremely rare event that any debris risk exceeds safety thresholds, the station performs a quick Debris Avoidance Maneuver (DAM) using thrusters to gently shift the orbit of the station and eliminate any chance of collision.

Physical Attributes

The station has a diameter of 1,000 meters and a length of 2,000 meters, supporting up to 3,000 residents with a comfortable, spacious living environment. The station was constructed using modular aluminum alloy panels that were manufactured on earth, packaged together, launched into orbit, and then assembled with robotic construction systems in the vacuum of space.
